The custody stack

One discipline, several organs: typed custody between observation, interpretation, authority, action, memory, and refusal. The map below is the system's actual architecture — the edges are the product.

Conversions — what must be earned, not assumed

live shipped · operational in daily use · research exploratory · zoned mapped, not built

An observation becomes a claim only through preflight that preserves its evidence.nq live
A claim becomes a premise only if it is admissible — policy engines decide over claims; custody decides whether those claims may become premises.wicket · agent_governor live
Standing observed is not standing spendable — the gap between check and exercise is measured on a typed clock basis, and a lapse refuses the spend.standing · agent_governor live
Drill, replay, and synthetic runs demonstrate structure; they cannot confer operational effect.agent_governor live
A verdict enters the evidence plane as a receipted claim, not a log line.agent_governor live
An authorized action still refuses without spendable capacity.linear accountant seam zoned
Logs are self-report until sealed.notary zoned
A lapsed grant must reach everyone currently relying on it, with delivery accounted.retraction transport zoned
