Operational

In daily use. Local-first, evidence-preserving, ops-shaped.

nq Claim preflight for operational systems. Separates observation from assertion, preserves evidence, interrogate with SQL. One binary, zero infrastructure.
nightshift Deferred agent work with receipts, reconciliation, and governed promotion. Schedules and resumes intent, not commands.
wicket Admissibility preflight kernel for agentic operations. One intent in, dimensional verdict and immutable receipt out. Heavy at the mint, light at the handle.
standing Standing and entitlement observability. Grant lifecycle, authority provenance, policy decisions, receipt chains.

Governance kernels & substrate

Standalone components that compose into the governance stack.

wicket-guard Admissibility preflight for AI-agent-authored diffs. Cooks authority-bearing surface mutations (LICENSE, NOTICE, CODEOWNERS, SECURITY) into Wicket intents and refuses unauthorized changes.
agent_governor Research substrate for governed agent work. Claims require evidence; actions leave receipts.
continuity Governed state persistence. Cross-session memory with observe/commit/rely semantics, hash-chained receipts, local SQLite.
verifier Formal admissibility sidecar using Z3. Proposals checked against grounded facts and constraint rules. Missing evidence is denial, not imagination.

ATProto / Bluesky

Transparency, monitoring, and moderation tooling for the AT Protocol.

rpp Receipts and reviewability overlay for ATProto. Structured claims, attestations, actions, challenges — rendered subject-centrically.
atproto-labelwatch Monitor labeling activity across the network, flag integrity-risk patterns. Live →
atproto-driftwatch Track how claims propagate and mutate. A labeler that doesn't emit labels.
atproto-labeler Custom labeling service with governed drift detection and a label decision ledger.
atproto-feeds Source-first signal desk. Structural feed ranking that rewards originality over engagement. Live →
atproto-archive Personal corpus archiver. Posts into durable, diffable JSONL + plaintext.
atproto-stats Follow noise analyzer. Interaction density, posting cadence, graph statistics across Bluesky.
